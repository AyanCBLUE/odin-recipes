Pasta Recipes

ingredients

  • pasta(any type can be macaroni or a spaghetti)
  • Onion with any types of veges you like
  • oil/butter
  • masala(like maggie magic masala) or tomato
  • Don't forget salt

Steps to create this Pasta recipe :-

  1. Boil in hot wated with salt and rinse it
  2. In a pan add butter/oil and heat it up
  3. Add some choped Onion or veges of your choise and slowly stir it
  4. Now add the pasta with a masala of your choice or make tomato paste
  5. Your Pasta is ready to be served
  6. (optional)You could add a bit of corriender leave or mint leaves

How to make a Kadak chai(tea)

Ingredients

  • tea leaves
  • ginger(adraak)
  • green cardamom(choti elaichi)
  • water
  • milk
  • sugar

Steps for creating an awsome tea(chai) recipe (for 2 cups):-

  1. Pore water(2 cups) on a container and put it on high flame
  2. when the water starts to boil up throw a figure nail amount of smashed ginger
  3. Now add the tea leaves(2 tablespoon)
  4. after 2min add 1 and half cup of milk and stir constantly
  5. When the milk starts to rise lower down the flame and stir for 1 min
  6. Now take 1 cardamom(elaichi) and smash it and then throw it in your tea
  7. Now pore the tea while filtering on a cup and enjoy a Kadak chai(tea)
